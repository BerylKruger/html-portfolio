Beryl's Favourite Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time

Serenity

This movie is the best mix of sci-fi meets western, meets action and adventure with some romance in the mix as well as a boatload of wit.

Forest Gump

I never get tired of rewatching this classic that was a pioneer in CGI. It is an endearing story of a very plain and unassuming man. From childhood to fatherhood, enduring various challenges without ever complaining of the cards life dealt him

Book of Eli

A man of faith traveling through a post apocolyptic world, on a mission to deliver the last copy of a very important book. The plot twist was amazing
